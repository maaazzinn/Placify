Placify

Your Gateway to Career Opportunities

Placify is a centralized platform that offers college students easy access to information about campus recruitment drives, internship opportunities, and other career resources. The auto-updating system simplifies the process of discovering and applying for opportunities, keeping students informed about relevant events, and deadlines.


The main goal of Placify is to empower students by not only providing career-related information but also offering insights into emerging technologies. The platform grants access to research papers on trending topics, promoting continuous learning and bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring students are well-prepared for their future careers.
